Frequently Asked Questions

How do you reach people?

You can use our tool "Find a location" to find the nearest communities on the map and hover to find out their information.

How can I volunteer?

You can register your interest by filling the contact form, an email will be sent to you with further details of our volunteeting programs.

Can I donate money instead?

We do encourage people and food places to donate food to local communities and reduce food waste.
