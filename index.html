We are Food Connections

Fighting hunger in every corner

Food connections is an initiative to help vulnerable individuals and communities fullfil their nourishment needs by having 3 meals a day, which often impossible due to their living conditions.
Food connections links food donors (e.g. restaurants, shops, farmers and other charities and organisations) to where the individuals or communities are located. Alternatively, they can reach our network of volunteers to assist them in finding and delivering the meals. Our mission is to help disadvantaged communities access food and necessities. Frequently Asked Questions

How do you reach people?

You can use our tool "Find a location" to find the nearest communities on the map and hover to find out their information.

How can I volunteer?

You can register your interest by filling the contact form, an email will be sent to you with further details of our volunteeting programs.

Can I donate money instead?

We do encourage people and food places to donate food to local communities and reduce food waste.
